Vision

Values
Intellectual independence, analytical rigor, and international collaboration.
  • 01
    Intellectual Independence
    We maintain complete independence in our research, free from political or institutional bias, allowing for honest and objective analysis.
  • 02
    Analytical Rigor
    Our research adheres to the highest academic standards, grounding every policy recommendation in solid data and evidence-based methodology.
  • 03
    International Collaboration
    We actively foster cross-border partnerships, believing that diverse perspectives yield stronger, more nuanced policy solutions.
  • 04
    Youth Empowerment
    We champion young voices at every level of global discourse, giving the next generation tools to shape the policies that will define their world.

The Process

How It Works

From national ambassadors to published policy briefs — a structured process designed to produce rigorous, actionable research.

01
Ambassadors

National Representatives

Country-level ambassadors coordinate research initiatives, recruit local talent, and represent WYC in national and regional policy forums.

02
Research Teams

Collaborative Groups

Small, multidisciplinary teams of young researchers collaborate across borders on focused policy topics, combining diverse academic backgrounds.

03
Case Studies

Real-World Analysis

Teams select and analyze real policy challenges, conducting primary and secondary research to understand root causes and evaluate existing approaches.

04
Policy Briefs

Actionable Recommendations

Research findings are distilled into concise, evidence-based policy briefs targeted at decision-makers, civil society, and the broader public.

Research Focus

Six priority areas where youth perspectives and rigorous analysis can meaningfully advance global discourse.

Technology & Society

Democracy & Institutions

National Research
Teams

Each national team is coordinated by a Country Ambassador and consists of researchers working on region-specific policy challenges. Teams collaborate internationally while staying rooted in their local contexts.

Our national teams are currently being established. Applications are now open — apply below to join your country's research team or become a national ambassador.
Open Role

Researcher

Join a national research team as an analyst and contribute to policy briefs, case studies, and collaborative research projects. Researchers work under the guidance of Country Ambassadors and connect with a global network of peers.

  • Contribute to internationally published policy briefs
  • Collaborate with researchers across multiple countries
  • Develop analytical and policy writing skills
  • Access to WYC's network and events
